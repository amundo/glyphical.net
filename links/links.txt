Manuel de Codage

https://xoomer.virgilio.it/omezzabo/Sinuhe/Sin_audio.htm
The Tale of Sinuhe
The Tale of Sinuhe, read aloud in Middle Egyptian with images of interlinear hieroglyphs and dramatic readings as mp3s.
literature audio text sinuhe

https://thotsignlist.org/
Thot Sign List
A project to document the uses/readings of hieroglyphic signs with references to original texts.
hieroglyphs database uses readings


https://archive.org/details/hieroglyphsofanc0000dods_l0z8/
The Hieroglyphs of ancient Egyptian
A full-color introduction to hieroglyphs with considerable detail and nice photos.
published:2001 book hieroglyphs overview free online no-download



https://app.vega-lexique.fr/
Vēga: Vocabulary of Ancient Egyptian
A database of Egyptian words. Alternate spellings, corpus citations. A bit confusing to use.
hieroglyphs database alternate-spellings part-of-speech

https://oraec.github.io/
ORAEC: Online Resources for the Ancient Egyptian Language
A large project to digitize and update Ancient Egyptian texts.
unicode github project corpus tei database
