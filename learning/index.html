Web Skills for Egyptologists

Patrick Hall

This is a little course in web skills for Egyptologists. The goal is to give you all the information you will need to create web content in or about Ancient Egyptian or Coptic.

Why, you ask? Well, why not? It’s fun to make stuff!

But also, there are a lot of creative people in the Egyptology community, and they volunteer to do all kinds of work to share what they know and what they’re learning. I wanted to find a way to share what I know about building to things in the web platform (not just the web, more about this distinction coming up…) to try to help that community make their work more sustainable in the future.
