About glyphical.net

This site is a work-in-progress with reference and learning material for learning Ancient Egyptian and especially Egyptian hieroglyphs.

I’m not an expert in Ancient Egyptian (though I have some degrees in Linguistics). I’m just a language nerd who likes to build websites.

I suggest posting in the community on Reddit at r/ancientegyptian if you have comments about this site, I check it often.
